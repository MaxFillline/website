Hello, World!

This is my personal website. I don't know what I'm going to do with it, but I'm excited. Check back to see how it develops.

I was a teenager when the web started creeping into homes via beige desktops with CRT monitors, dial up modems, and floppy disks. The web was much more personal then and I miss that. I'm here claiming my corner of the internet to try and bring back some of that feeling.
